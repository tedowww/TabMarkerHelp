📌Mark a tab: Control(⌃)+[0-9] or Ctrl+[0-9] Double-Tap

🌏Switch to a marked tab: Control(⌃)+[0-9] or Ctrl+[0-9] Single-Tap

🗑️Unmark a tab: Control(⌃)+D or Ctrl+D

📋View marked tabs: Shift(⇧) Double-Tap

🔗Open bookmark URL: Option(⌥)+[0-9] or Alt+[0-9]

⚠️Shortcuts do not work on system pages (chrome://newtab, etc.)
